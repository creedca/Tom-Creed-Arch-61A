712 Broadway

New York

Brief

This loft in the centre of Greenwich was one of the last ‘functioning’ lofts in the village. It was being used as a machine shop and required a complete gut renovation. The brief was to convert the space into a comfortable 1-2 bed loft apartment for a single occupant.


Design Concept

The design was influenced by the client’s love of nature and natural materials. All the materials used in the retrofit were locally sourced, including red oak and bluestone from upstate New York. The series of level changes implemented in the design allowed a spatial progression. This was achieved with a 14’ high vaulted ceiling in the front living space overlooking Broadway, which continues all the way through the loft to the master bedroom. As one moves to the back of the apartment, it progressively becomes a more anthropometric ‘scaled’ space, providing an intimate and private context.
